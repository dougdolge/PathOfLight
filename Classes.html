Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga

Intended for begginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of Yoga, this 60 minute class of poses and slow movement focuses on asana (proper alignment and posture), Panayama (breath work) and guided mediation to foster your mind and body connection

Vinyasa Yoga

Although designed for intermediate to advanced students, beginners are welcome to sample the 60 minute class that focuses breath synchronization movement - you will imhale and exhale as you flow energetically through yoga poses.

Restorative Yoga

This 90 minute class features very slow movement and long poses that are supported by a chair or wall. This calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any levrel of experience. This practice can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate an injury.

Relax anytime with Savasana


Prepare yourself for savasava. Lie down on your yoga mat with your palms up. Close your eyes breath slowly but deeply. Sink into the mat and let your worries slip away. When you are ready, roll on your side and use your arms to push yourself into a sitting position with crossed legs. Place your hands in a prayer position. Be grateful for all that you have in life. Namaste
